Proving TAPL in Coq (ch 4, WIP)

December 21, 2019 One-minute read
tapl
coq 
Inductive term : Type :=
| term_true
| term_false
| term_cond (cond t1 t2 : term)
| term_zero
| term_succ (n : term)
| term_pred (n : term)
| term_iszero (n : term).
